# Adidas-Sales-Performance-Dashboard

Business Problem

Adidas operates across multiple regions, product categories, and sales channels. Understanding sales performance and profitability is critical for identifying growth opportunities, optimizing inventory allocation, and improving commercial decision-making.

This project aims to analyze Adidas sales data and develop an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor revenue performance, profitability, and market trends.

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance and profitability.
- Identify top-performing products and regions.
- Evaluate sales performance across channels.
- Discover trends and business opportunities through data visualization.
- Provide actionable recommendations for business growth.

## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains:

9,648 sales transactions
Multiple sales regions
Product categories
Retailer information
Sales channels
Revenue and profit metrics

## Dashboard Features
### Executive KPIs
- Total Sales
- Operating Profit
- Units Sold
- Average Sales per Transaction
### Product Performance Analysis
- Best-selling products
- Revenue contribution by category
- Profitability comparison
### Regional Analysis
- Sales by region
- State-level performance
- Geographic sales distribution
### Sales Channel Analysis
- Online
- In-store
- Outlet
### Time Series Analysis
- Monthly sales trends
- Seasonal performance patterns

## Key Insights

### Revenue Concentration
A significant portion of revenue is generated from a small number of top-performing regions, indicating strong geographic concentration.

### Product Performance
Certain footwear categories consistently outperform other product segments and contribute significantly to total sales revenue.

### Channel Effectiveness
Sales performance varies across online, in-store, and outlet channels, highlighting opportunities for channel-specific marketing strategies.

### Seasonal Trends
Sales fluctuate throughout the year, suggesting opportunities for demand forecasting and inventory optimization.

## Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory allocation for top-performing product categories.
- Prioritize marketing investment in high-growth regions.
- Strengthen digital sales channels to capture additional market share.
- Utilize sales forecasting to improve inventory planning and reduce stock imbalances.
- Implement region-specific sales strategies based on local performance patterns.
